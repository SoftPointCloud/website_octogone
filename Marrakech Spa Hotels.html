Inspired by the area's pristine natural habitat...

Our concept was inspired by Balinese massage which focuses on the use of different oils including, a ginger mix, sugar cane mix, coffee bean mix, etc.

Sultry Spa offers a complete range of treatments, in addition to, a unique octagon-shaped Hammam. The Hammam is the Middle Eastern variant of a steam bath. It played an important role in cultures of the Middle-East, serving as places of social gathering, ritual cleansing, and as architectural structures with special customs attached to them. Sultry is centered on a coed octagon-shaped Hammam setting, allowing our clients treatments within the relaxing hypnotizing steam.   

We specialize in over twenty-five different therapeutically and inspirational massages. We train our staff and masseuse to make our environment welcoming and at the same time relaxing. Our services range from the traditional to our signature eight hand work down which will leave you wondering how you did without. With a focus on holistic principles and non-invasive techniques, our spa offers a series of inventive and restorative treatments, facials and therapies that provide a sense of well-being and tranquility, paving the way to a more balanced living.
